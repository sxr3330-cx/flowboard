THE PROBLEM

Paper flows lose rounds.
We fix that.

01
Illegible. Unsynced. Slow.
Paper flows are scribble your partner can't read, on a different page than you, with no record of what was extended three speeches ago. You're losing arguments before you make them.
02
One shared flow, live.
Flowboard gives you a real-time collaborative flow — one sheet per position, starting at the right speech. Both partners see every argument, extension, and kick the moment it happens.
03
Stop dropping arguments.
The respond button opens a linked card in the next speech. The speech doc builds from what you click. The 2AR that wins is the one flowing on Flowboard.

FEATURES

Everything built for the round

Every feature in Flowboard was designed around a specific moment in a debate round.

01
Real-Time Sync
Both partners see every change the moment it's typed. Firebase-powered, sub-second latency, any device.
02
Flow Sheets Per Position
One sheet per position. Neg flows start at 1NC. Aff flows start at 1AC. No wasted columns.
03
Respond System
Click respond on any arg — a linked response card opens in the next speech column, auto-scrolled and focused.
04
Live Speech Doc
Shared speech panel. Flow Access mode lets you click any arg to pull it into your speech as a formatted block.
05
Evidence Search
Search OpenEv and OpenCaselist evidence natively. Upload HSPolicy 25-26 cards to search them inline.
06
Extend to Any Speech
Click extend, pick the target speech. A copy appears marked EXT. Your notes carry over automatically.
07
Kick Entire Flows
Kick a position — Politics DA, Cap K — with one click. The whole sheet crosses out for both partners live.
08
Cross-Apply
Link arguments across flows. Cross-apply badges show connections and clicking them scrolls to the linked arg.
09
Spectator Mode
Room creator controls who can edit. Spectators see everything live. Toggle access from the presence row.
10
Undo / Redo
Full undo and redo history. Ctrl+Z and Ctrl+Shift+Z. Every edit is reversible without leaving the flow.
11
Persistent Rooms
Room codes are permanent. OCTAS2026 today is the same room in a month — all flows, all args, still there.
12
Google Sign-In
Sign in to save room history. Recent rooms appear in your profile with one-click rejoin.

ABOUT

Built by debaters,
for debaters

Every policy debater knows the feeling. You're in the 2AR. You know the neg dropped the agriculture solvency extension in the 1NR. But your paper flow is three pages of scribble and your partner is pointing at a different column. You go for it. The judge doesn't buy it.

"The flow is the round. If you can't read it, you've already lost."

Flowboard is the platform we wished existed. Real-time shared flows, a speech doc that builds from what you click, and card search that lives inside the tool. No account required. No setup. Open a room, share the code, and flow.
The respond system came first — click respond on a 1AC arg, a linked card appears in the 1NC. The kick feature came from watching neg teams forget they read the cap K. The speech doc came from watching 2ARs fall apart because the debater was on paper instead of the flow.
